Target Audience

Our target audience is anyone who lives in our community who needs to know about the weather for planning purposes. They feel a part of our community and enjoy it.

Persona 1: Mom

    30-55 years old
    2.5 kids
    goes out for many activities and errands
    interested in how to dress for the weather

Persona 2: Dad

    30-55 years old
    2.5 kids
    goes out for daily commute
    concerned with morning and evening commute drive times
    interested in weather for family activities, outings and yard work

Scenarios:

    Mom wants to know today's weather for her workout-- Mom comes to the homepage, checks out the morning forecast and looks at the local events feed
    Dad wants to know what to expect for his drive in to work-- Dad comes to the homepage and clicks the link for drive time weather
    Mom wants to check on the forecast for the kids' soccer game-- Mom comes to the homepage and drills down into the weather map radar overlay view
    Dad wants to know if the baseball game will get cancelled due to weather-- Dad comes to the homepage and looks at the evening forecast
    Mom wants to know if she should wear her nice shoes to work-- Mom comes to the homepage and looks at the 12-hour outlook
